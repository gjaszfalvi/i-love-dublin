Culture

Dublin offers a variety of things to do, when it comes to your free time. No matter if you are a hobby musician or simply a tourist who wants to discover Ireland's nature. Here are some things you might haven’t heard of before.

Game of Thrones Tour


You might know that parts of the games of Thrones series is filmed in northern Ireland. There are various scenes and locations that can be visited. The Games of Thrones Bus Tour is starting in Dublin and stops at almost every single location. Enjoy Winterfell, Westeros and Tyrion and Jon’s campfire on their journey north to the Wall, for a great price of 55€.

Musicmaker Sale


This secret is for all the musicians. Musicmaker is one of the several music shops in Dublin Center. A huge sale is going on at least once a year where you have the chance to win awesome prizes and get your preferred gear for 20-80% of the original price. This is a huge event where people sleep in tends to be first in line. It will be addressed on music makers Facebook page.

Visit Website

Troubadour Rehearsal Studio


If you play in a band or want to find one you will need to rehearse. The troubadour rehearsal studio is by far the best place to play music. You can rent a room for 5 hours, which is equipped with a full drum set, amps and vocal PA for only 60€.
