Career

Finding the right job can be a hassle, if you don’t know where to search or even where to be found. We’ll give you a hint for the best places to go to. And remember, education does not have to be expensive if you manage to do it the smart way.

Irishjobs & Monster


Find a job with Irishjobs and Monster. Both platforms connect you with potential employers or recruitment agencies. You can upload your CV and cover letter to be recognized by potential employers without even contacting them yourself.

Irishjobs.ie Monster.ie

Springboard+


You want to study full time or part time and don’t know how to finance the tuition fees? Springboard+ offers a choice of over 280 free, part-time and intensive conversion courses in higher education from certificate, to degree, to postgraduate level. It does not matter if you are already working, want to change career or are unemployed. Avoid taking loans and keep your hard earned savings by applying for course via springboard.

National College of Ireland


The national college of Ireland is based in the heart of Dublin. You can study full- or part time. NCIRL offers a variety of full-time and part-time courses in the areas of business, finance, psychology and computing. Luas and Dart are nearby and you’ve provided with food from several shops and restaurants around. It is not only convenient to study at NCIRL but also a great choice in in terms of small class sizes, friendly atmosphere And the experienced faculty.
