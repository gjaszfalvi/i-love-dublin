Budgeting

Money can be a problem for new arrivals. Make sure you don’t pay more than necessary for goods and services. There are always cheap alternatives that are not advertised heavily or used by the majority of people. It is worth knowing a few of them.

Permanent TSB


Save yourself the hassle of going to a bank to open a personal account. Permanent TSB makes it as easy as possible to open a current account via phone and post only. And the best thing: You won't pay quarterly fees if you have a monthly income of at least 1500€.

Adverts & Donedeal


You want to get rid of your stuff? You are not sure if your newest hobby will last longer than 3 weeks? Visit Adverts. is to buy and sell all kinds of second hand goods from motorboats to furniture. And in case you can’t find it there, go to Donedeal.ie, which is a similar platform. Just avoid Gumtree because of its scammers from the UK.

Visit Adverts Visit Donedeal

DeliverMe


The online shop you want to order from is not delivering to Ireland? No worries, "deliverme.ie" is a shipping service based in Newry (behind the border), forwarding your delivery from UK to Ireland for a fair price of 22€ per 25kg and parcel.
