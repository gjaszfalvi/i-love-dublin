Mobility

Mobility and the way you want to commute and get your shopping done is probably really important for you. The choice on how to travel will have a huge influence on the time you will spend on the road and the costs associated with it. You might find out that you do not necessarily need a car as there are a number of cost effective alternatives available.

Gocar


The sharing economy came to Ireland with a car sharing service named Gocar. Register as a member and rent a car or even a small van for only 5-6€ per hour, plus a kilometer fee of 0.45€. Just book your car online and collect it from one of the numerous Go-bases all around Dublin. Being a member costs you 5€ per month, being fully ensured only 100€ a year. Book online and open the car by swiping your member card on the window.

Hail-o


Dublin seems to have more taxis than Ireland has sheep's. However, not all of them provide a good service. Especially newbies do often get overcharged by taxi drivers. The Hailo-Taxi-App is perfect for a secure drive and your payment can be made via credit card connected to the app. The driver receives a signal from you, no matter where you are. Check the displayed map and see which Hailo cab is close to your location.

Visit Website

Leap Card


Travel the smart way with Dublin’s prepaid card for train, bus and tram. The leap card can be charged on every train or luas ticket counter and allows you to avoid buying single tickets all the time. Get your own leap card and save up to 20-27% when commuting. And you’ll save even more if you are a student.

Visit Website

Airlink 747


A Taxi from city center to the airport can be really expensive. The alternative is the Airlink, a bus service that brings you to the airport for an incredible price of 6€. It runs every 15 minutes during rush hour and stops at most major places in Dublin’s center.

Visit Website

BoxyMo Car Insurance


Not only young drivers, but also foreigners who have never been insured in Ireland before, are usually forced to pay a higher car insurance fees. Boximo installs a little device in your car that measures your driving. A commendable driving style gets rewarded with a lower insurance fee.
