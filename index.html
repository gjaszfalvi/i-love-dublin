I love DUBLIN

--- lifehacks for newcomers ---


I-Love-Dublin.com is a static lifestyle guide that helps readers/visitors who live in Dublin to design their daily routine efficiently and economically. Especially foreigners who move to Dublin like both of us did, do sometimes miss out on basic information regarding housing, shopping, mobility and money. We help them avoiding beginner mistakes, like choosing the wrong bank or getting screwed by a landlord. However, this site is not only made for new arrivals, there are plenty of information on it for people who live in Dublin since years. The page is basically a collection of links to external websites of companies and organizations.
