Housing

Your home might be the most important aspects of your life, since it is a basic need to live in a clean and nice environment. That gives you security and increases your quality of life. Since most newbies are tenants for years and might even have to share an apartment for a while with people they don’t know, it can be an advantage to know the little tips and tricks that improve your living situation.

Hassle.com


The number 1 cause of trouble in a shared living space is caused by inmates ignoring the cleaning arrangement. Avoid unnecessary confrontations with your flatmates or spouse by hiring a professional cleaner for only 12€ an hour. Hassle.com is a quality online platform where every listed cleaner can be rated. A supportive customer service will assist you with bookings, schedules and all your cleaning related concerns.

Ikea Delivery


You need new furniture and don’t have a car to bring it home? No problem, Ikea offers a delivery service for only 35-95€ depending on your purchase amount. Order online and avoid being trapped in traffic and the endless Ikea labyrinth.

Tesco Delivery


There is absolutely no need to use a car and your pressures time for grocery shopping. Tesco.ie offers a delivery service for your groceries. Just order everything from bread to washing powder online, save your basket and choose a delivery slot and any day from 7am-11pm and from 10am-10pm on Sundays. The delivery itself will only cost you 4-7€.
